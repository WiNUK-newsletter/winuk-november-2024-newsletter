HELLO WINUK COMMUNITY,

We couldn’t open this month’s newsletter without mentioning our inaugural WiNUK Awards. This was the perfect opportunity to celebrate the amazing and hard-working members of the WiNUK community, and everyone who has supported us throughout the years. WiNUK founder, Elizabeth English, wrote a letter to personally thank all of you for the support shown:

"WiNUK Awards 2024 was a huge success! Bringing together over 100 attendees, across our in-person event at the Department of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge, and our livestream. We celebrated inspiring women neuroscientists from undergraduates to senior researchers, trailblazing sex/gender neuroscience research, and vital work towards inclusive neuroscience, highlighting mentors and communicators. A huge thank you to the incredible Women in Neuroscience UK volunteer team for making this event so smooth. It was amazing to have 20 of us there in person, after years of virtual collaboration. Special mentions also must go to our expert judges, keynote speaker, Dr Flaminia Ronca, and our amazing sponsors - this event was made possible through your collaborative support! Here’s to 2025, as we look to continue our collective efforts towards more inclusive neuroscience, for women and for all." ~ Lizzie
